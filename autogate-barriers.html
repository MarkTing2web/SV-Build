Vehicle Access Fundamentals

1. Introduction to Autogate & Barrier Systems

Vehicle access control is the first line of defence for any landed property, condominium, or industrial facility in Singapore. A well-engineered system balances security with traffic flow efficiency.

Point of Entry: Intelligent barriers manage the flow of authorised vehicles.

Modern vehicle access systems have evolved beyond basic motors and remotes. Today's architectures integrate seamlessly with building management systems, security clouds, and Singapore's ERP Infrastructure through IU readers.

Whether you are managing a single residential driveway or a high-traffic commercial loading bay, the principles of motor torque, safety sensing, and secure identification remain the foundation of a reliable installation.
