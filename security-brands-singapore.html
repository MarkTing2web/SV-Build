Our Technology Partners

20 authorised brands across 5 system categories — specified for Singapore's property and security requirements.

Securevision does not sell brands. We specify them. Every brand we carry has been evaluated against Singapore's climate, regulatory environment, and integration requirements. We recommend the right brand for the right application — not the most expensive option, and not whatever is in stock.

Surveillance & Detection Partners

For surveillance, we partner with four brands that cover the full spectrum — from cost-effective IP cameras for landed homes to enterprise-grade AI analytics platforms for large commercial and institutional sites. Every brand we specify integrates natively with our chosen VMS platforms and supports the AI analytics capabilities that define modern surveillance.

Gate Motor & Barrier Partners

Vehicle access is a mechanical as much as an electronic challenge. Gate motors and boom barriers must handle Singapore's humidity, duty cycle demands, and safety requirements — a gate that fails open is a security breach, and a gate that fails closed is an operational crisis. We specify brands whose mechanical engineering matches the electronic integration.

These brands are deployed as part of our Vehicle Access & Control system design. → Learn how we design vehicle access systems

FAAC

Why we specify:

Our primary recommendation for automatic gate motors and boom barriers across all property types. FAAC's Italian engineering delivers the duty cycle reliability and torque control that Singapore's high-use condominium and commercial sites demand. Their barriers integrate cleanly with our GantryGo vehicle management platform and LPR systems.

High duty cycle Italian engineering GantryGo integration Condominium-proven
View FAAC Singapore →

MAG

Why we specify:

A reliable mid-range gate motor option for residential properties — landed homes and smaller developments — where duty cycle demands are lower and cost efficiency matters. MAG motors deliver consistent performance for single and double leaf swing and sliding gates.

Residential fit Swing and sliding Cost-effective Reliable
View MAG Singapore →

Dormer

Why we specify:

Our recommendation for heavy-duty industrial and high-security gate applications — large sliding gates, high-frequency access points, and industrial facilities where the gate motor must handle significant weight and volume without maintenance issues.

Heavy-duty Industrial grade High frequency Large gates
