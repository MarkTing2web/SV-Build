We Work the Way You Work

Different professionals need different things from a security subcontractor. Here is what we provide for each.

For Architects

Technical specifications in the format your M&E drawings require. Device locations marked on your floor plans. As-built drawings on completion. We work to your specification brief and flag anything that needs to be resolved before walls close — not after.

For Interior Designers

Timeline coordination around your handover date. Equipment and device aesthetics that match your design intent — we do not install visible cable trunking on finished surfaces without your agreement. Full client handover training so your client knows how to use what we installed.

For Main Contractors

We show up on the agreed date. We complete our scope on time. We do not create problems for other trades. We produce a pre-wiring schedule before construction starts and verify the electrical work before plastering — catching issues when they are still easy to fix.

Deliverables

What Working With Securevision Looks Like in Practice

Not promises — deliverables.

Proposals within 48 hours

Send us your floor plan and client brief. We return a detailed proposal with line-item pricing, system specifications, and a preliminary timeline within 48 hours. If you need a budget number faster, call us and we will give you a range on the phone.

Pre-Wiring Schedule

Before construction starts, we produce a pre-wiring schedule your electrician can follow: cable types, conduit routes, device locations, and power point specifications. This is not a rough guide — it is a document your contractor can work from.

Rough-In Verification

We visit site before plastering to verify that cable runs match our schedule. If something is wrong, we catch it now — when fixing it takes an hour, not a week.

Installation to Your Timeline

We schedule our installation around your handover date. If your timeline changes, we adapt. We do not push your handover date.

Full Documentation Package

On completion: as-built drawings, system manuals, zone lists, user guides, and warranty documentation — everything your client needs in a single handover package.

Client Training and Support

We conduct a full training session with your client on the day of handover. We provide our direct contact details for follow-up questions — so those calls come to us, not to you.

The Project Timeline

From your first call to client handover — what each stage looks like and when we are on site.

1

Initial Brief

You send us floor plans and a brief — security requirements, client priorities, budget range. We return a detailed proposal within 48 hours.

2

Pre-Wiring Package

We produce the pre-wiring schedule and coordinate with your M&E contractor before construction starts. If needed, we attend a pre-construction coordination meeting.

3

Site Stages

Rough-in verification before plastering. Attendance during false ceiling installation if device positions need marking. Final installation after tiling and painting are complete.

4

Handover

Full system commissioning, testing, and client training. Documentation package issued. Your client has our contact for support. You are not their first call when the alarm beeps.
