The Problem

The Danger of Siloed Security

In most modern buildings, security subsystems (CCTV, Access, Alarms) operate in isolation. This creates critical operational gaps during an incident.

The Silo Effect

An alarm triggers on the 4th floor. The guard must manually find the right camera, search for the timestamp, and cross-reference access logs. Critical minutes are lost in navigation.

The Integrated Solution

With VESTA™, an alarm automatically pops up the relevant camera feed, highlights the offender's access record, and locks down adjacent zones — all in one click.
