System Architecture

What Systems Do Commercial Properties Need?

Every commercial property has unique operational needs, but most offices, retail stores, and buildings benefit from three core security pillars. Here's how we architect security infrastructure for Singapore's commercial sector.

Surveillance & Detection

ESSENTIAL

AI-powered cameras that do more than record. Our systems provide loss prevention, customer analytics via heat mapping, and POS integration to correlate transactions with footage.

Typical components:
  • 10-60+ IP cameras
  • AI analytics (loitering, crowd detection)
  • Heat mapping & customer flow analysis
  • POS transaction integration (retail)
Learn More About Surveillance →

People Access Control

ESSENTIAL

Facial recognition or mobile credentials eliminate lost keycard issues. Visitor management kiosks automate guest registration and contractor access with full audit trails.

Typical components:
  • Facial recognition or mobile access
  • Visitor management kiosks
  • Temporary contractor credentials
  • Staff mobile app integration
Learn More About People Access →

Vehicle Access & Barriers

OPTIONAL

For properties with parking: automated gantries with ANPR for staff season parking, visitor vehicle tracking, and delivery management to reduce entry congestion.

Typical components:
  • Automated gantries (1-3 entry/exit)
  • ANPR staff vehicle recognition
  • Visitor vehicle registration
  • Delivery vehicle time-limited access
Learn More About Vehicle Access →

Platform Integration

ESSENTIAL

Unified VESTA™ dashboard for retail chains and multi-tenant buildings. Single login for centralized monitoring, incident logging, and real-time facility manager alerts.

Typical components:
  • VESTA™ unified platform
  • Multi-site / multi-store management
  • Centralized monitoring & reporting
  • Remote access for facility managers

What Affects the Final Cost?

Five factors determine your commercial security investment:

  1. Property size and locations: Larger spaces and multi-site retail chains require more cameras and centralized infrastructure.
  2. Operational complexity: High-traffic retail needs more sophisticated analytics than low-traffic office spaces.
  3. Integration requirements: Connecting to POS, building management, or HR databases adds scope.
  4. Peak traffic patterns: Managing arrival congestion or lunch rush may require additional access points.
  5. Premium features: Advanced AI analytics, facial recognition, or heat mapping adds value.

Our site assessment provides a transparent, fixed-price proposal with clear line items for finance team approval. No hidden costs, no surprises.

Compliance & Standards

Built for Singapore Commercial Standards

Every commercial property in Singapore operates under workplace safety, fire safety, and data protection regulations. We engineer systems that protect your business, comply with statutory requirements, and reduce operational liability.

SCDF Fire Safety Compliance

All electronic access systems comply with SCDF codes. Emergency breakglass at all doors ensures safe evacuation. Optional auto-release configuration during fire alarm activation.

What we provide:
  • Emergency breakglass at all doors
  • Fire alarm integration (optional)
  • Evacuation route compliance
  • SCDF inspection documentation

Required for all commercial properties with electronic access. Critical for occupant safety.

Workplace Safety & Health (WSH)

Installations by bizSAFE Level 3 certified technicians following MOM guidelines. We ensure proper cabling containment and electrical safety to minimize hazards.

What we provide:
  • bizSAFE Level 3 installers
  • Proper cable containment
  • Electrical safety compliance
  • Risk assessment documentation

Essential for businesses managing employee safety obligations under Singapore's WSH Act.

Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA)

Customer footage and employee records are protected under PDPA. We architect systems with encrypted storage and role-based access for authorized personnel only.

What we provide:
  • Encrypted data storage
  • Role-based access controls
  • Automatic retention policy
  • PDPA-compliant audit trails

Mandatory for retail and commercial properties handling customer and employee data.

BCA & Electrical Safety Standards

All installations comply with BCA regulations and Electrical Wiring Standards. We coordinate with LEW for proper routing and surge protection for fit-out works.

What we provide:
  • LEW coordination & certification
  • BCA submission for fit-outs
  • Electrical safety compliance
  • Proper cable containment

Essential for new fit-outs, major renovations, and tenant improvement works.
