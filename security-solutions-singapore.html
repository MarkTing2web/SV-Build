Self-Diagnostic Guide

4 Questions Before You Choose

01

What are you protecting?

Are you safeguarding people (family, staff), physical assets (inventory), or digital infrastructure?

02

Where are vulnerabilities?

Are the entries (gates, loading bays) or internal blind spots the main risk area?

03

Who manages security?

Does the owner decide, or do you need MCST/Committee approval with third-party guard integration?

04

Budget & Timeline?

Are you looking for a planned upgrade or correcting an immediate critical system failure?
