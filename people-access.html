Why It Matters

Know Exactly Who Is in Your Building

Traditional locks and keys give you no audit trail, no revocation control, and no way to know who accessed what and when. Modern access control transforms your building's entry points into intelligent, managed checkpoints.

Securevision designs people access systems that scale — from a single front door to a 500-unit condominium with multiple zones, resident access levels, and visitor pre-registration. Every entry is logged, every credential can be revoked remotely.

  • Complete access event log for audit, compliance, and investigations
  • Multi-factor options: card + PIN, card + biometric, mobile
  • Visitor management with time-limited credential issuance
  • Zone-based access levels — restrict areas by role or time
  • Integration with CCTV for visual verification at every access point
  • Remote management — add, modify, or revoke access instantly

Access Hardware

Every Access Point, Engineered

From pedestrian turnstiles and biometric readers to intercom stations and visitor kiosks — we specify and install the right hardware for every entry scenario.

🚪

Turnstiles & Entry Barriers

High-throughput pedestrian barriers for lobbies, transit hubs, and facility entrances. Options from basic tripod to full-height security turnstiles.

  • Flap, swing & tripod types
  • Full-height security turnstiles
  • Speed gate for high traffic
  • ADA-compliant wide lanes
  • Bi-directional control
💳

Card Readers & Keypads

RFID and smart card readers for standard door access. Proximity, Mifare, DESFire, and multi-technology options for interoperability.

  • Mifare & DESFire encrypted
  • Multi-technology readers
  • Indoor & outdoor rated
  • Card + PIN combination
  • Tamper detection built-in
👆

Biometric Terminals

Fingerprint, facial recognition, and palm vein readers for high-security access points. Eliminates credential sharing and buddy punching.

  • Fingerprint (1:N matching)
  • Facial recognition (AI-based)
  • Palm vein scanners
  • Liveness detection
  • Mask-compliant face recognition
🔐

Electric & EM Locks

Fail-safe and fail-secure locking mechanisms for every door type — glass, timber, metal frames. Integrated with fire alarm for emergency release.

  • Electromagnetic locks (EM)
  • Electric strikes & drop bolts
  • Fail-safe / fail-secure options
  • Fire alarm integration
  • Door position sensors
👤

Visitor Management Systems

Digitise the visitor journey from pre-registration to badge printing to access credential issuance — replacing paper logbooks with a secure digital trail.

  • Web & mobile pre-registration
  • QR code visitor passes
  • ID document scanning
  • Time-limited access credentials
  • Host notification via SMS/email
📞

Intercom & Video Door Stations

Audio and video communication between visitors and residents or security. Integrated with access control for remote door release from any device.

  • IP video door stations
  • Indoor monitor units
  • Mobile app door release
  • Multi-tenant support
  • CCTV snapshot on call
Credential Options

The Right Credential for Every Context

We design access systems around how your users actually operate — balancing security with convenience at every access point.

💳
Standard

RFID / Smart Card

Durable, cost-effective credentials for large user populations. Encrypted Mifare or DESFire cards prevent cloning.

📱
Modern

Mobile / BLE

Smartphone-based credentials via Bluetooth or NFC. No physical card required — ideal for tech-forward developments.

👆
High Security

Biometric

Fingerprint or face — the credential is the person. Eliminates card loss, credential sharing, and buddy punching.

🔢
Backup

PIN Code

Keypad entry as a standalone or second-factor. Ideal for after-hours access, visitor PIN codes, or emergency override.

Applications

Access Control for Every Property Type

Condominium

Multi-Zone Resident Access

Card or mobile access for unit doors, lift lobbies, gym, pool, and carpark. Visitor intercom with remote release from resident smartphones.

Office

Corporate Office Access

Role-based access levels — reception open, server room restricted, executive floor multi-factor. Full audit log for compliance and HR investigation.

Industrial

Hazardous Zone Control

Biometric access to restricted production areas. Shift-based time zones, contractor credential management, and automated headcount reporting.

Institution

School & Campus Control

Student, staff, and visitor credentials with time-based zoning. Automatically lock classrooms outside school hours. Integration with attendance systems.

Healthcare

Clinical Zone Management

Ward access restricted to credentialed staff. Pharmacy and drug storage with biometric + PIN dual verification. Patient protection zones logged.

Multi-tenancy

Mixed-Use Buildings

Separate access domains for each tenant while sharing infrastructure. Building management retains override — tenant admins manage their own staff.
