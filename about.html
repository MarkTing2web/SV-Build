About Securevision

Built by Experience.
Driven by Engineering.
Focused on Smarter Security.

Securevision is a Singapore security systems specialist founded in 2006 by Ler Wee Meng, an engineer and business leader with over 35+ years in the security industry. We help property owners, managing agents, and businesses design smarter, more connected security environments that go beyond standalone products.

Our Story Talk to Us
Founder’s Story

Why Ler Wee Meng Started Securevision

Most security companies sell equipment. Securevision was built to solve real security problems properly — with clearer thinking, better design, and long-term accountability.

After years in the security industry, Ler Wee Meng saw the same problem repeated across many sites: systems were being installed, but they were not always designed around the client’s actual risks, operations, or long-term needs.

He saw alarms that triggered without clear visibility, camera systems that recorded incidents but did little to prevent them, and installations that looked complete on paper but were weak in real-world use.

That frustration led to the founding of Securevision in 2006.

From the start, the company took a different approach: assess the site carefully, understand how the property actually operates, recommend the right combination of systems, and remain accountable long after handover.

Today, Securevision serves clients across residential, commercial, and industrial environments in Singapore, with a focus on practical design, system integration, and long-term support.
