The GantryGo™ Rental Model: Zero Capital Risk

Our breakthrough solution for security companies — rent gantries monthly, relocate when contracts end.

How GantryGo™ Works:

❌ Old Model (Capital Purchase)

  • Buy gantry: $30-50k upfront
  • If contract lost → equipment stuck
  • Cannot relocate easily
  • Capital at risk

✅ GantryGo™ Model (Monthly Rental)

  • Rent gantry: Monthly fee (no upfront capital)
  • Contract lost → Relocate to new site
  • Transfer to another contract
  • Zero capital risk

3 Exit Options When Contract Ends:

  1. Relocate: Move gantry to your new contract site
  2. Transfer: Hand over to incoming security company (they take over rental)
  3. Return: End rental, no further obligation
