The Competitive Landscape

Estates Are No Longer Choosing Guarding Companies and Technology Integrators Separately.

The estate security market in Singapore has shifted. MCST committees and managing agents are looking for solutions where the guarding and technology layers work as one integrated solution.

Guarding companies that walk into a tender with a credible technology partner already in place are presenting something meaningfully different from companies offering manpower alone.

This is not about replacing guards. It is about the guarding company being able to demonstrate that their guards are supported by the right systems, trained on the right platforms, and backed by a technology partner with the credentials and SLA to keep those systems running.

What the Partnership Delivers

Three Reasons Guarding Companies Work With Us

Win Better Contracts

A joint proposal that integrates guard deployment with technology coverage is a stronger tender than manpower alone. We give you the technical depth to compete for integrated contracts.

Make Your Guards More Effective

AI alerts tell them where to respond before they investigate. Pre-registered visitors arrive without queue. Digital handover means the team starts every shift fully informed.

Keep Tech Complaints Off Your Guards

When a gantry jams, the escalation goes to us under a written SLA. Your guards stay focused on guarding. We handle the technology resolution.

The Platform Your Guards Will Use

VESTA — What Your Guard Team Sees and Does on Every Shift

VESTA is the operational platform designed specifically for on-site security teams. It removes the friction of paper-based logbooks and clarifies escalation paths.

  • Visitor Logging: Digital records immediately visible to estate management. No transcription.
  • Pre-registration Checking: Integrated ANPR means expected visitors arrive without guard intervention.
  • Incident Reporting: Logged in real-time, time-stamped, and searchable by the committee.
  • Technology Fault Reporting: Faults logged in VESTA escalate directly to Securevision's SLA team.
