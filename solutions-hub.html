Our Approach

Why Property Type Matters

A camera is a camera, but a system design for a warehouse is fundamentally different from one for a luxury home.

Residential Focus

  • AI Human Detection for garden perimeters
  • Mobile alerts with zero false positives
  • Video intercoms with smartphone routing
  • Minimal cabling and discreet hardware

Condominium Focus

  • Centralized Server (VMS) architecture
  • Lift integration & floor access control
  • Visitor Management (VMS) with QR codes
  • Maintenance SLAs for guardhouse uptime

Commercial Focus

  • Cloud-based remote management
  • Heatmaps and visitor analytics
  • Integration with building management
  • 24/7 cloud backup of critical footage
