Self-Diagnostic Guide

4 Questions Before You Choose

Answer these honestly. They’ll tell you exactly what you need.

1

What are you protecting?

  • People (family, staff)
  • Assets (inventory, data)
  • Operations (reputation, compliance)
"Protecting people? Access control first. Protecting assets? Surveillance + perimeter."
2

WHERE ARE THE VULNERABILITIES?

  • Entry points (gates, doors, loading bays)
  • Blind spots (no cameras, no coverage)
  • Weak controls (lost cards, shared codes)
"Walk your property. Where can someone get in undetected?"
3

WHO MANAGES SECURITY?

  • You (owner decides, fast implementation)
  • MCST/Committee (AGM approval, 3-6 months)
  • Security company (guard integration needed)
"This determines timeline and approval process."
4

WHAT’S YOUR BUDGET & TIMELINE?

  • Budget ($10K? $50K? $500K?)
  • Urgency (broken system vs planned upgrade)
  • Phasing (start critical, expand later)
"You don't need everything at once. You can build in phases."
