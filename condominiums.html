System Architecture

What Systems Do Condominium Estates Need?

Every condominium estate requires all four integrated security pillars working together. Here's how we architect security infrastructure for MCST-managed properties, from boutique developments to large-scale estates.

Surveillance & Detection

ESSENTIAL

Estate-wide camera coverage for lobbies, corridors, car parks, and perimeters. AI-powered analytics to detect loitering, unauthorized access, and vehicle tracking. Centralized recording for MCST compliance and incident investigation.

Typical components:
  • 50-200+ IP cameras (multi-block coverage)
  • Car park & corridor coverage
  • AI analytics (loitering, tailgating detection)
  • Cloud backup for MCST audit trails
Learn More About Surveillance →

People Access Control

ESSENTIAL

Resident, visitor, contractor, and delivery personnel management. Integrated video intercoms at every unit. Temporary access for cleaners, renovation contractors, and service providers. Complete audit trail for MCST AGM reporting.

Typical components:
  • Resident access cards (RFID/NFC)
  • Video intercom systems (per unit)
  • Visitor registration kiosks
  • Contractor temporary access management
Learn More About People Access →

Vehicle Access & Barriers

ESSENTIAL

Season parking holder recognition via ANPR. Visitor vehicle tracking and time-limited access. Delivery vehicle management. Integration with guard house systems to eliminate manual logbooks and reduce visitor congestion.

Typical components:
  • Multiple gantries (2-4 entry/exit points)
  • ANPR season parking recognition
  • Visitor vehicle registration & tracking
  • GantryGo™ integration (automated logs)
Learn More About Vehicle Access →

Platform Integration

ESSENTIAL

Unified VESTA™ dashboard connecting surveillance, access control, and vehicle management. MCST-ready audit reports for AGMs. Multi-block coordination. Remote monitoring for managing agents.

Typical components:
  • VESTA™ unified platform
  • MCST audit report generation
  • Multi-site/multi-block management
  • Guard handover automation
Learn More About Platform Integration →

All four pillars are essential for condominium estates. Unlike private homes, estates require platform integration to meet MCST audit requirements and coordinate security across multiple blocks and entry points.

What Affects the Final Cost?

Five factors determine your estate's investment:

  1. Estate size and unit count: Larger estates require more cameras, access points, and cabling infrastructure.
  2. Number of entrance points and gantries: Each vehicle entry/exit point requires dedicated gantry systems and ANPR cameras.
  3. Existing infrastructure condition: Upgrading legacy systems or working with old cabling increases complexity.
  4. MCST compliance requirements: Some estates require enhanced audit trails, cloud backup, or specialized reporting.
  5. Facilities integration: Connecting pool access, gym entry, BBQ bookings, and function room management adds scope.

Compliance & Standards

Built for MCST Compliance & Singapore Regulations

Every condominium estate in Singapore operates under strict regulatory frameworks. We engineer systems that meet MCST audit requirements, protect resident privacy, and comply with all relevant safety and building standards.

MCST Security Audit

Complete documentation for security audits. Every access event, vehicle movement, and system incident is automatically logged with timestamps for MCST board transparency.

What we provide:
  • Automated audit trail generation
  • Incident reports for board review
  • Security expenditure tracking
  • Maintenance log documentation

Required for all MCST-managed properties. Essential for board accountability.

PDPA Data Protection Compliance

Resident data and CCTV footage are protected under PDPA. We architect systems with encrypted storage and role-based access controls for authorized personnel only.

What we provide:
  • Encrypted data storage
  • Role-based access controls
  • Automatic retention policy
  • Access audit trails

Mandatory for all estates. Protects MCST from legal liability under PDPA.

SCDF Fire Safety Compliance

All door access complies with the Fire Code to have Emergency Breakglass. Where needed, the system can be integrated with the fire alarm to release doors during a fire evacuation.

What we provide:
  • Fire-code compliant door access
  • Emergency breakglass protocols
  • Optional fire alarm integration
  • Evacuation route compliance

Required for all estates with electronic access. Critical for resident safety.

BCA & Electrical Safety Standards

All installations comply with BCA regulations and Electrical Wiring Standards. We coordinate with LEW for proper cable routing, grounding, and surge protection.

What we provide:
  • LEW coordination & certification
  • BCA submission for major works
  • Electrical safety compliance
  • Proper cable containment

Essential for new installations and major system replacements.
