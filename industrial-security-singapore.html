Our Philosophy

Security is Not a Product. It’s a System.

For critical industrial infrastructure, security cannot be an afterthought. We believe true resilience comes from an engineering-first approach. We bridge the gap between heavy-duty operations and intelligent protection by designing security into the very fabric of your facility.

The 3 Foundations of Modern Industrial Security

We move beyond basic surveillance to build integrated systems that protect your perimeter, harvest intelligence, and ensure workforce safety.

Perimeter Resilience

Operational Intelligence

What Affects the Final Cost?

Six factors determine your industrial security investment:

  1. Perimeter length: Extensive outdoor areas require more cameras and detection systems.
  2. Number of operational zones: Separating production floors, hazmat storage, and server rooms increases complexity.
  3. Regulatory requirements: WSH, SCDF, MOM, and industry-specific regulations add documentation scope.
  4. Operational hours: 24/7 facilities require redundant systems and zero-downtime installation.
  5. Vehicle throughput: High-volume hubs need more sophisticated ANPR and loading bay coordination.
  6. Mission-criticality: Data centres require thermal detection and multi-factor authentication.

Compliance & Standards

Built for Singapore Industrial Regulations

Every industrial facility in Singapore operates under strict workplace safety, fire safety, and operational standards. We engineer systems that protect your workforce and reduce operational liability.

WSH (Workplace Safety & Health)

AI analytics monitor PPE compliance and restricted zone access. Complete incident documentation for WSH audits and safety reporting.

What we provide:
  • AI PPE compliance detection
  • Restricted zone access enforcement
  • Incident documentation for audits
  • bizSAFE Level 3 certified installers

Mandatory for all manufacturing and logistics facilities. Critical for reducing workplace accidents.

SCDF Fire Safety Compliance

Integrated access systems for emergency egress. Thermal imaging for fire-risk hotspots in server rooms and substations.

What we provide:
  • Fire alarm integration (auto door release)
  • Thermal fire-risk detection
  • Emergency evacuation compliance
  • SCDF inspection coordination

Required for all industrial facilities. Especially critical for data centres and chemical storage.

MOM & Contractor Safety

Temporary contractor credentials and visitor escort requirements. Automated audit trails for incident investigations and reporting.

What we provide:
