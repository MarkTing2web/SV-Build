What Affects the Final Cost?

Six factors determine your healthcare security investment:

  1. Facility size and resident population: Larger populations require more covering and robust systems.
  2. Number of zones: Multi-building campuses need coordinated response protocols.
  3. Regulatory requirements: MOH, MOM, and PDPA compliance add documentation and hardware scope.
  4. Resident welfare features: AI wandering and fall analytics increase system complexity.
  5. Operational throughput: High-density dormitories need high-speed face recognition.
  6. Emergency preparedness: Occupancy tracking and alerts add critical safety layers.
