We Understand Your Position

You Are Accountable to Every Committee, Every Resident, and Every System — Simultaneously.

Managing agents in Singapore carry a weight that most people outside the industry do not fully appreciate. You are simultaneously accountable to multiple MCST committees, responsible for hundreds of residents across different estates, and expected to make informed decisions about technical systems you were never trained on.

Security systems are one of the most consistent sources of friction. They fail at inconvenient hours. They generate resident complaints. They create documentation burdens before every AGM. And when something goes wrong — a gantry jammed at midnight, an intercom down during a long weekend, a CCTV system that loses footage during an incident — you are the first call, regardless of which integrator is supposed to be managing it.

Most integrators are set up for installation. Their business model is project-based — they win the contract, install the system, and move on. The relationship that follows is reactive: you call when something breaks, they come when they can.

We operate differently. Our service model is built around the MA relationship — because we understand that your ability to manage your estates effectively depends on having an integrator who is genuinely accountable to you, not just to the scope of the last contract.

Reliable 24/7 accountability for your portfolio

Our Service Commitment

What Our SLA Looks Like in Plain Language

Every estate we maintain operates under a written SLA with three response tiers. This is what you should expect from any professional integrator — and what we commit to in every contract.

Tier 1 — Critical

Complete Failure

Gantry failure blocking access, intercom failure at main entrance, or complete CCTV outage.

2hr Acknowledgement
4hr On-site Response
24/7 Availability
Tier 2 — High Priority

System Degradation

Partial camera outage, single access point failure, or visitor management system issues.

4hr Acknowledgement
Resolved within 1 Business Day
Tier 3 — Standard

Minor Issues

Single camera maintenance, software configuration adjustments, or firmware updates.

1 Business Day Response
3–5 Business Day Resolution

Every maintenance visit and fault call is documented in writing — your protection for MCST audit requirements.
