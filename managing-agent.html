We Understand Your Position

You Are Accountable to Every Committee, Every Resident, and Every System — Simultaneously.

Managing agents in Singapore carry a weight that most people outside the industry do not fully appreciate. You are simultaneously accountable to multiple MCST committees, responsible for hundreds of residents across different estates, and expected to make informed decisions about technical systems you were never trained on.

Security systems are one of the most consistent sources of friction. They fail at inconvenient hours, generate complaints, and create documentation burdens before AGMs. When something goes wrong — a jammed gantry or a CCTV outage — you are the first call.

We operate differently. Our service model is built around the MA relationship — because we understand that your ability to manage your estates effectively depends on having an integrator who is genuinely accountable to you, not just to the contract.

Reliable 24/7 accountability for your entire estate portfolio.

The MA Service Model

Built Around Accountability, Not Just Installation

Operational Support

Written SLA With Tiered Response Times

Critical faults acknowledged within 2 hours, on-site within 4 hours, 24/7. Standard faults within 3–5 business days. Defined in writing.

Named Project Manager Per Estate

Every estate has a named Securevision project manager. You always know who to call — not a generic support hotline.

Remote Diagnosis Before Site Attendance

We identify whether it is a power, network, or hardware failure remotely, saving hours of downtime.

Documentation and Reporting

Bi-Annual Preventive Maintenance

Cleaning, firmware updates, and battery testing with a written health report provided after every scheduled visit.

MCST Audit Reports on Demand

Access summaries, incident logs, and expenditure tracking generated directly from VESTA for your AGM presentations.

End-of-Life Notification

We notify you 18 months before a major component reaches manufacture end-of-life, allowing for planned CAPEX advice.

Our Service Commitment

Our SLA in Plain Language

Every estate we maintain operates under a written SLA with three clear response tiers. This is the professional standard we commit to in every contract.

Tier 1 — Critical

Complete Failure

Gantry failure blocking all access or complete CCTV/Intercom outage.

Acknowledged in 2 hours
On-site in 4 hours
24 / 7 Response
Tier 2 — High Priority

System Degradation

Partial camera outage or single access entrance point failure.

Acknowledged in 4 hours
Resolved in 1 business day
Tier 3 — Standard

Minor Maintenance

Software configuration or individual camera adjustment.

Acknowledged in 1 business day
Resolved in 3–5 business days

Every maintenance visit and fault call is documented — your protection for MCST audits.
