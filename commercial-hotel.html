Large-Scale Security for Hotels & Commercial Buildings

Comprehensive facility-wide security for hotels, commercial buildings, and mixed-use developments. From guest safety to mass surveillance to emergency response.

Request Facility Assessment See Common Challenges

Challenges Building & Facility Managers Face

Managing security across large facilities requires more than cameras — it requires strategy, integration, and scalability.

🏢

Large Premises, Multiple Zones

Lobbies, floors, loading bays, car parks, F&B outlets — each needs different security coverage.

👥

High Visitor Volume

Guests, tenants, contractors, deliveries — need to track who's where without bottlenecks.

📹

Mass Surveillance Needs

100+ cameras required. Storage, monitoring, retrieval must be efficient and reliable.

🔐

Multi-Level Access Control

Staff, guests, contractors need different access levels. Can't give everyone master keys.

🚨

Emergency Response

Fire, medical, security incidents — need instant notification to right people.

🔗

System Integration

Security must integrate with BMS, PMS (hotels), fire systems, lift access.

Enterprise-Grade Security Solutions

Scalable, integrated systems designed for large facilities and high-traffic environments.

📹

Mass Surveillance (100+ Cameras)

Enterprise VMS. AI analytics. 60-90 day storage. Remote viewing. Incident search.

🔐

Multi-Zone Access Control

Different access levels by zone, time, role. Integration with lift access and parking.

👥

Visitor Management at Scale

Digital check-in kiosks. QR pre-registration. Host notification. Contractor tracking.

🚨

Emergency Notification

Mass notification to security, management, fire services. Zone-specific alerts.

🚗

LPR & Car Park Management

Automated vehicle tracking. Season parking. Visitor parking alerts. Blacklist enforcement.

🔗

BMS/PMS Integration

Connect security with building management, hotel PMS, fire systems, lifts.

Our Facility-Wide Implementation Process

1

Site Survey

Walk entire facility. Map zones. Identify vulnerabilities. Count entry points.

2

System Design

Camera placement plan. Access zones. Integration requirements. Scalability.

3

Phased Installation

Minimal disruption. Floor-by-floor or zone-by-zone. Night shifts if needed.

4

Training & Handover

Security team training. Management dashboards. Standard operating procedures.
