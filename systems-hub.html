III. Design Standards

Engineering for the Singapore Environment

Our standards aren't global; they're localized. From BCA compliance to tropical weatherproofing, we engineer systems that survive and thrive in Singapore's unique infrastructure.

SCDF Fire Safety Logic

Door Access control is more than just a badge reader. We ensure all electromagnetic locks integrate with the Fire Alarm Isolation Module (FAIM) for fail-safe emergency egress.

IP67 + Tropicalization

Singapore's rain intensity is 100mm/hr+. We specify IP67+ weatherproofing with anti-corrosion mounts for all outdoor sensors and barrier actuators.

MAS TRM Compliance

For our financial and institutional clients, we implement systems that exceed Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) Technology Risk Management (TRM) guidelines.

V. The Action Step

The Security Architect's Self-Audit

Perform a high-level technical assessment of your current infrastructure. If you answer "No" to more than two of these, your current system is likely creating significant liability.

01
Incident Continuity

If your internet connection fails, does your local NVR / Controller continue to log events and store footage without interruption?

02
Power Redundancy

Is your control panel backed by an Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) capable of at least 4 hours of operation?

03
Data Integrity

Can you produce a timestamped incident report with a unified audit log (CCTV + Access Control) in under 5 minutes?

04
Firmware Compliance

Has your systems' firmware been updated in the last 6 months to protect against known cybersecurity vulnerabilities?
