Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you do site assessments before quoting?
Yes — for any system installation, we conduct a site visit to assess coverage requirements, existing infrastructure, and risk zones before providing a proposal. There's no charge for assessments on new system projects.
How quickly can you respond to a service call?
For clients on a maintenance contract, we target same-day or next-business-day response for service calls. For ad-hoc breakdowns, our call-out fee starts from $80 and we'll give you an estimated arrival time when you contact us.
Can you service systems you didn't install?
Yes — we service and maintain most major brands including Hikvision, Paradox, DSC, RISCO, ZKTeco, FAAC, and more. WhatsApp us the brand and model and we'll confirm serviceability before you arrange a visit.
What areas of Singapore do you cover?
We cover all of Singapore island. Our team is based in Geylang East and typically serves the east, central, and north-east regions fastest — but we do not restrict coverage by area.
Do you offer maintenance contracts?
Yes — we offer maintenance programmes for CCTV, alarm, door access, intercom, auto gate, and barrier systems. Contracts include scheduled preventive maintenance visits, priority response, and discounted parts.
Are you licensed to do security work in Singapore?
Yes. We hold Police Licence L/PS/000267/2023P, issued under the Private Security Industry Act. This licence is a legal requirement for security systems installation in Singapore and is not easily obtained — it requires background checks, technical qualifications, and ongoing compliance.
