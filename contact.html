Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you do site assessments before quoting?

Yes — for any system installation, we conduct a site visit to assess coverage requirements, existing infrastructure, and risk zones before providing a proposal.

How quickly can you respond to a service call?

For clients on a maintenance contract, we target same-day or next-business-day response. For ad-hoc breakdowns, we'll give you an estimate when you contact us.

Can you service systems you didn't install?

Yes — we service and maintain most major brands including Hikvision, Paradox, DSC, RISCO, ZKTeco, FAAC, and more.

Are you licensed to do security work?

Yes. We hold Police Licence L/PS/000267/2023P, issued under the Private Security Industry Act. This is a legal requirement for security systems installation in Singapore.
