The Challenge

Managing Safety Across a High-Footfall Public Space

SCAPE is one of Singapore's busiest youth activity hubs, hosting events, F&B, and recreational facilities across multiple levels. The previous surveillance system was fragmented, had significant blind spots, and could not provide management with real-time situational awareness.

  • Analog cameras with low resolution and poor low-light performance
  • No AI analytics — all monitoring was manual, reactive
  • Incident response time averaged 15 minutes from detection to action
  • Multiple blind spots on stairwells, service corridors, and event spaces
  • No centralised management — each floor managed separately
  • High foot traffic made manual monitoring ineffective during peak hours
The Solution

200-Camera AI System with Real-Time Crowd Intelligence

Securevision designed and deployed a 200-camera IP surveillance network with embedded AI analytics — covering every floor, entry point, stairwell, and event space. All feeds centralised on a VESTA™ management platform with proactive alert logic.

  • 200 IP cameras with AI behaviour analytics across all floors
  • Real-time crowd density detection and alert thresholds
  • Loitering, perimeter crossing, and abandoned object detection
  • Centralised VMS with floor-by-floor overview and alert feed
  • Incident auto-logging — camera clip + timestamp + alert type
  • Mobile management — security team responds from anywhere on-site

AI Camera Network

200 Hikvision IP cameras with embedded deep-learning AI. 4K resolution at key zones, 2MP at coverage zones. Fully integrated analytics.
