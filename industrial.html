We Design Industrial Security Systems — Not Just Install Cameras

Your facility doesn't need "cameras on a fence." It needs layered perimeter security, inventory protection, loading bay monitoring, and workforce safety — all integrated into one intelligent system.

❌ Basic Camera Thinking

  • • "Put cameras on the fence"
  • • "Add some floodlights"
  • • "Get a guard to watch screens"
  • • No perimeter intrusion detection
  • • No inventory monitoring, no analytics

✅ Layered Security Strategy

  • • Perimeter intrusion detection (AI alerts)
  • • Loading bay monitoring (prevent theft)
  • • Inventory protection (high-value areas)
  • • Workforce safety (restricted zones, incidents)
  • • 24/7 remote monitoring with instant alerts

That's why we conduct site surveys to understand your operations — not just count how many cameras you need.

Common Industrial Security Challenges

Large facilities, high-value inventory, and 24/7 operations create unique security requirements.

🚧

Perimeter Security

Large fenced areas (acres). Need to detect intrusions instantly. After-hours trespassing. Theft over fences.

📦

Inventory Theft

High-value goods, raw materials, finished products. Internal theft (employees, contractors). Loading bay blind spots.

🚚

Loading Bay Monitoring

Can't see what's being loaded/unloaded. Driver verification needed. Prevent unauthorized pickups.

⚠️

Workforce Safety

Restricted areas (machinery, chemicals). Incident documentation. Lone worker safety. Emergency response.

🌙

24/7 Operations

Facilities run around the clock. Need constant monitoring. Can't staff guards everywhere.

🔍

Large Area Coverage

Acres of land to monitor. Multiple buildings, yards, outdoor storage. Camera blind spots.
